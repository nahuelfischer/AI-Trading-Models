# Weather Data

All my thoughts about the usage of weather data.

## Corn

### Location
As already mentioned in the crop data journal I will use weather data of these 11 states:
1. Iowa
2. Illinois
3. Nebraska
4. Minnesota
5. Indiana
6. South Dakota
7. Kansas
8. Missouri
9. Ohio
10. North Dakota
11. Wisconsin

They have the most impact on US corn production and all lie in about the same area.
Based on these states I selected 6 weather stations:
- Des Moines
- St. Louis
- Rochester
- Indianapolis
- Kearney (Nebraska)
- Aberdeen (South Dakota)

These should provide enough regional coverage to train the model on historical data and current data for inference.
